About

Capital Valley Islamabad

Capital Valley Islamabad stands out as an exceptional housing society in the twin cities due to its strategic location, ensuring easy accessibility from major points of the city.
While the project offers a plethora of facilities, it recognizes the crucial importance of its convenient positioning to provide a truly unmatched real estate opportunity.

Capital Valley Islamabad enjoys an incredibly convenient location, being situated zero kilometers away from the New International Airport Islamabad.
Moreover, it is just a short drive from the main Kashmir Highway and Zero Point Islamabad. This prime positioning places major landmarks within easy reach, making it a highly accessible and desirable destination for residents and investors alike.

The execution of the Capital Valley Islamabad project is undertaken by..

The Capital Valley Islamabad project is carried out by Capital Valley, a company with a proven track record of successfully completing various construction and development ventures in the past. The town planning responsibility for Capital Valley Islamabad lies with Urban Solutions Pvt Ltd, a reputable firm with extensive experience in the real estate market of the country. Urban Solutions Pvt Ltd has been entrusted with development consultancy for prominent names such as DHA, CBR, The Grand City Kharian, and Fazaia Housing Scheme. Additionally, Green Thumb is actively involved in creating the landscape design for the society, ensuring that Capital Valley Islamabad will offer an exceptional amount of green spaces compared to other housing societies in the region.

SKB is a multinational engineering and construction firm with a wide range of interests and abilities. They offer a comprehensive range of services across various countries, including Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, Oman, Dubai, Kenya, and Malaysia, and are also expanding their operations to other regions in Asia, the Middle East, and Africa.

Urban Solutions is the firm defining the life styles of Pakistanis for tomorrow, by designing their communities today! We are the leading urban planning and architectural design consultancy firm of Pakistan. We can cater to any demand, from designing a small lot in a walkable catchment to the master planning of an entire region, from envisioning smart growth to realizing transit-oriented developments. The Urban solutions is providing the best services for physical master planning, and ensuring the urban future of Pakistan is secured.
